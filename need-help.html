Need help?

How we can help

Warriors of Warmth enables community spaces to remain warm and operational. We provide materials, including boilers, pipework and radiators, and coordinate teams of qualified engineers who volunteer their time to install complete heating systems for local venues that serve their communities across England.

What we do

  • Provide all materials required for community heating installations
  • Coordinate teams of qualified engineers who volunteer to install full heating systems
  • Concentrate on shared community spaces that bring people together
  • Support organisations that strengthen community connection

Who we're here for

Our support is directed at community organisations that require heating to operate or deliver activities. We work with shared spaces such as community halls and local groups, not individual households. If you are unsure whether your project is eligible, please contact us to discuss.

Nominations

While we cannot support every project, we welcome all enquiries. If you represent a community space in need, or wish to nominate one, please use our contact form. Every nomination is reviewed so we can identify projects where we are best placed to help.

You may nominate your own organisation or another. Please provide as much detail as possible to help us assess how we might assist.

Where we operate

Our service operates across England. We work with communities and charities in all regions.

How we run

Warriors of Warmth is a registered non-profit. All materials are donated by partner brands; all labour is provided voluntarily. Tradespeople and volunteers give their time so that community spaces can stay open and warm.
